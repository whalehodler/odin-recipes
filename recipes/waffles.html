Waffles

Description

This is a basic waffles recipe for breakfast.

The only difference from regular waffles is that it is made using tvorog, which makes them nice and tender.

Ingredients:

  • 200gr Tvorog
  • 2 Eggs
  • 120gr AP
  • 1tsp Baking powder
  • 2Tbsp Sugar
  • 6Tbsp Milk
  • 50gr Butter
  • Spices to taste

Steps:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl together with sugar. Whisk until light foam is formed and sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add Tvorog in. Mix.
  3. Add Milk. Mix again.
  4. Melt butter, but don't overdo it. Cool it a bit, add to a bowl and mix.
  5. Add AP with baking powder to the bowl, mix thoroughly.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  7. Heat the waffle machine. Add in the mass we got.
  8. Close the waffle machine andcook for 4 to 6 minutes.
  9. Add some topping, fruit, berries or heavy cream and consume.
